Extract themes, pain points, and opportunities from raw user research notes.

How this works
Claude is prompted to act as an analyst, not a summarizer. The difference matters: summarization just condenses what's there, while analysis identifies patterns, weighs frequency, and surfaces opportunity areas that aren't explicitly stated in any single response. The structured output format (themes, pain points, opportunities, gaps) maps directly to the artifacts a PM needs before a discovery readout.
