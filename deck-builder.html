What I chose and why

  • Conclusion-first structure. The deck forces you to lead with the recommendation, the way consulting decks do — not the narrative buildup most product decks default to.
  • Live preview while Claude drafts. Slides render as they generate, so you can spot a wrong angle in slide 2 instead of waiting for slide 14.
  • Browser-only export to PowerPoint. Uses pptxgenjs client-side — no server, no upload, no IT-approval blockers when an exec needs the deck in 30 minutes.
